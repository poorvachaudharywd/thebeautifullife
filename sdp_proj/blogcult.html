Shopping spree in foreign land

Don't miss these items in tourists bazaar.


Let's start with Paris. Here, there are no surprises that you'll find the best of fashion accessories ranging from lingerie to hats and handbags. You'll also get best chocolates and candies here which can be taken as a souvenir.


Ever wondered where can you get those Macadamia nuts that Ross was chewing. Well, the answer is Hawaii. Also, quilt, shirt, necklaces as well as Kona coffee which is pretty famous.


In Japan, you can buy all the Kitkat bars with variety of flavours. Here, kimonos and cosmetics are also well known.


If you're in New Zealand, Whittaker's chocolate is pretty famous after Cadbury. NZ wines are legendary which can gifted as well. Choc fish, well it sounds fishy but is actually pretty amazing fish-shaped marshmallow chocolate.


Rome is actually known for it's italian ceramics and clothing items such as shoes, jewelry, silk scarves. Perfume and pasta can also be brought back as souvenir.


You will get the best of spices and arabian dates in Dubai. Pashmina shawls, traditional arab shoes, precious stones and of course Arabic attars are also some og the items you can get here.
